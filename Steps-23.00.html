Goals Geographical Ranking (Filled Map - Bottom Right) & Legend 23
  1. Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select Filled map.
  2. Fill up fields: Location=Team, Continent, Tooltips=Total Goals, Goals WOW %,Goals Preventions WOW %, Cards WOW%, Shot Attempts WOW %.
  3. For Format  visual: Map settings: style= Road, show labels=on, controls: auto zoom= on, zoom button= off. fill colors: use below screen shot (colors for rule1=#a1343c, rule2=#eb895f, rule 3=#e1c233.


5. For Format  General: Properties, Size, Height=331, Width=669, Position, Horizontal= 831, Vertical=569. Title=On, Text=Goals Geographical Ranking, , Font=Segoe UI(17),Bold, Text color= #FFFFFF, Background color=#a1343c  , Horizontal alignment=Center, Effects: Background=On, Background color= Whit, Transparency=0%. visual border :color = whit, rounder corners = 0px.
6. To do the map legend, go to insert tab, click on image and select the Map_Legend and click open.
7. In the format pane, go to general and enter the bellow properties: Height=49, width=121, horizontal=873, vertical=838, title=off.
8. To do the map legend text indicator, go to the insert tab, click on text box, click inside the box and enter 'HIGH  LOW', font=Segoe UI, bold(10), Left aligned , color=#a1343c(LOW),#a38600(HIGH).Effects: Background=Off.
